My Works

A collection of projects I've worked on

Sugar CRM – Customer Journey Dashboard

Sugar CRM delivers a comprehensive dashboard that empowers businesses to visualize customer journeys, track leads, and automate workflows. With rich data analytics and a highly intuitive UX, it supports efficient and scalable CRM strategies.

Velobank – Digital Neo Bank Card Landing Page Website

Velobank is a landing page website concept created for a digital neo-banking app. The project explores a modern visual approach, placing emphasis on clean design, simple onboarding, and brand trust for tech-savvy users.

InterviewX: AI-Based Business Model Consulting Platform

InterviewX helps startup leaders establish strategic clarity through AI. It uses data-driven persona modeling and business verification to provide tailored insights, empowering better decisions and supporting rapid growth across industries.
